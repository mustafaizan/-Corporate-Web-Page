How the Process Works?

The Japanese language level is N5 (beginner) - N1 (highest).
First of all, we provide training on alphabet and reading to you, dear Japanese enthusiasts, just like in primary school.
We can describe the N5 level as literacy. Many of our tutors completed this phase quickly, learned with fun and loved Japanese.
We know that after N5 level, you will quickly step into N4-N3 levels. We will train you in daily conversations, slang, Japanese pronunciation of foreign names etc.. Our trainings include Japanese Linguistics teachers.
We also start to offer you vocational training after N3 level.
